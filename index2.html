ATTRACTION


In addition to 21 amusement park rides, The Park at Aptech features a great selection of traditional Midway games plus Wacky Waters, a new, interactive splash pad that makes Aptech cooler than ever!





RIDES


Our 21 dazzling amusement park rides will have everyone entertained for hours. Play it safe on Leap Frog or see how loud you scream on the Rollin’ Thunder! With attractions for guests of all ages.




TICKETS


Get a head start on your adventure by purchasing your tickets before you get to the park! We have a broad range of ticket packages and prices available for purchase.






RESTURANT


There are several options for dining in the Merciado amusement park as well as cart vendors throughout. Shady Arbor and Veranda restaurants are centrally located and offer a variety of both food and beverage to quench your thirst, cool off and refuel.
